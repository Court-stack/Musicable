Why I am studying

What is your interest in IT:

I'm studying IT in order to change career paths. I've always had an interest in computers and modern technology but had never really pursued it. I have no educational or professional background in IT except for short time in high school where I had learnt some basic HTML. Why did you choose to come to RMIT:

I chose RMIT because it always seemed like such a prestigious university. I would see the campus whenever I travelled to Melbourne and would think to myself “I’d love to be a student there.” I had to decide on where I wanted to study IT, the only local university is the University of Tasmania which isn’t a bad university, but it certainly isn’t the best around. So, I made a choice to study online instead and saw that RMIT did online classes, this lead me to applying for the foundational courses here.

What do you expect to learn from your studies:

How to present myself and my ideas in a professional way suitable for a career related to the IT field.
